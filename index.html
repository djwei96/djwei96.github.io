PhD · The University of Hong Kong

I hold a PhD from the Faculty of Business and Economics at HKU and previously earned my master's degree at the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I also served as a visiting scholar at the Alfred Lerner College of Business & Economics, University of Delaware.

My research focuses on generative AI, LLMs, trustworthy AI, healthcare, and data mining — bridging information systems and computer science to tackle real-world problems in business and society. I will be joining ESCP Business School (Paris) as Assistant Professor in Fall 2026.
